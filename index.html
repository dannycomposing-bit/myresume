About
Background & setup

Danial Ekteshafy is a composer working at the intersection of electronic precision and physical weight — writing music that treats dread, doubt, and clarity as textures to be mixed, not themes to be illustrated.

The work draws on IDM's rhythmic fracture, math rock's structural logic, and modern metal's density, held together by long ambient beds underneath. The aim is always the same: something that sounds inevitable, not decorated.

Composing first, with editing and mixing as extensions of the same instinct — shaping a piece until nothing is left that isn't doing something.
